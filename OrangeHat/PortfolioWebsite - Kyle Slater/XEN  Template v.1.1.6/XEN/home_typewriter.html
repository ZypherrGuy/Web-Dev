I AM ZYPHERR
DEV / ESPORTS

I would label myself a Hobbyist of multiple interests. From thinking out the box and applying a creative touch, to getting my hands dirty in some late-night coding sessions. My life is centered between two of my greatest passions - Technology and Esports. I have always been passionate about the competitive side of gaming but, to my luck, my gene pool lacked the skills required to compete at a competitive level. I have always wanted to be an integral

part of the road that leads to the complete construction of an Esports empire, both inside and outside of South Africa. If I wasn't going to grow Esports as a player, I was going to apply my skills I was confident in and do it another way. Being a part of Esports and gaming organizations over the past 7 years has given me clarity on the opportunities this industry has to offer and how I can help Esports, both locally and internationallu, reach its recognized potential.
